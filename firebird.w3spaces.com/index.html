About FireBird



About Us

FireBird is a website devoted to helping developers write more efficient, smaller and understandable code, by either using web servers for backend (or frontend) implementation, or by using a some of our multitude of libraries to preform or simplify a generally complicated task (or in some cases, simple but you are too lazy). Our services are completely free, as this project is open source.

What do we offer?

FireBird offers a large range of different libraries in a multitude of programming languages, which can do lots of different things ranging from server communication to data structuring to string tokenisation and formatting - we cover practically all bases. We also have a multitude of cloud services, including FireCloud, a massive collection of different online scripts, servers, files and even web pages which are all available for usage.

Does this cost anything?

All FireBird (and FireCloud) services are completely free to use, as this project is entierly open-source, and also comprised of parts of other open-source software. In other words, no, you don't have to pay a signle thing!!

Data and Privacy

We respect everybody's privacy greatly, and therefore we do not sell your data, or share your data with any third party for marketing. Our services also do not collect, use, access or require any personal/confidential data either, therefore making you pretty safe!!
